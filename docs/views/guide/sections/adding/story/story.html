The next thing you'll want to do is set a story to use the Foil format. To do this, you'll need to have a story created and then follow these steps:

1) While editing the story, open the story menu by clicking on the story's name in the lower-left-hand corner of the screen.

2) From this menu, you will want to click the "Change Story Format" option. This should bring up a menu called "Story Format".

3) From this menu, click the square to the left of the Foil format and close the menu.

Your Tine 2 story is now using the Foil format. You can now utilize all of the features of Foil from within your story.
